A killer stalks our manor house
So a sleuth has come to call
To rakes us through the coals and make
The killer take the fall.

The case at once had run aground
With cash and sexual runaround;
Despite a maid, uncovered, bound,
Still the motive stayed unfound.

Now we sit in crackling study
And hear a tale complex and bloody.
I thrill at the fact that
The time is now; oh, heaven, heaven,
How your words will make us feel.
I did it.
I killed her.
I love a big reveal.
