I am an insane scientist;
I've chosen to eschew
Morals, reasons, sense and fears
And blasted peer review.
I've raised the unclean dead, it's said,
And bred malarial mice
Whose poison stings and leather wings
Killed Helsing in a trice.

My grand abode, although quite vast
Will never feel like home
For now Elizabeth's gone away
I only feel alone.

-Viktor Frankenstein
