Press pen to pad with restless dreaming
In bid to quell my metric daemon.
For one shall find that one is blest
When meter's met and one may rest.

I claim as my scene body politic in sense of modern youth.  In sense of
Reddit and Twitter, of Brothers Koch and noxious light shone
Down exposing social cracks.

Whatever happened to the ladies that voted?
That took note of the day and goings-on?
I charge them with the eternal crime:
They grew up.

Why care to vote when the men in charge are
Bastards, thieves, lacking gumption or merit,
Lacking even power.  When Google shows us the world
Then tells us that the world avoids their tax.

Facebook, now, necessary for any advertorial excursion
To the sharp exclusion of those disconnected,
Tells us that they avoid their tax.

Amazon, the goldmine on which Internet novelist's dreams
Are based, even if everyone agrees their workers pay should be raised
Tells us that they avoid their tax.

The Rich may speak and tweet and beat the Taxman back
While the Real find ourselves contained into a hostile walled garden.
Why vote?
Why not smash instead - remind the scum
That the angry youth aren't stupid - just dumb.
