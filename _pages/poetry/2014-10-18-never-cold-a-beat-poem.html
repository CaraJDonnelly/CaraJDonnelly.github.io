Underfoot the black tarmac clicks
And scurries all around.  The ground -
Roach covered - sways beneath
The breeze.  You stand, sickly as
A diamond, with no colour in
Your cheeks.  I burn
And clench myself inside,
While you simply stand
Waiting.

Your roaches have penetrated the jet
By which I arrived.
They trip the laser switch
And we all die together
In a
Petroleum
(cymbal)

Based
(cymbal)

Explosion.
(Tapping on the high-hat)

Revenge is a dish best served
Indiscriminately.
