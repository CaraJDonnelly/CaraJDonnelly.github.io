In Modern Times, in ill-run schools
There are some things they fail to mention.
A case in point: Teaching the rules
Of verse...

...like Rhyme, or Stress, or (for the sake of Pity!) Scansion.
We hear "The Greats - they paid no heed
To edicts of styles gone past."
False! False, for the factual case, in deed, was that

...you'd chime with me.

For I find fine verse is so much sweeter
When meter's met; it is then meeter.
