This is a list of troupes Cara has seen, organised by location.  This list is an aid to memory only, and does not list temporary teams.

London

  • Anne and Frank - Carleen MacDermid and Shem Pennant (both of C3467X), monoscene
  • Austentatious - Jane Austen narratives
  • Bleak Mouse - Harold team that came from a Monkey Toast class
  • Breaking and Entering - Maria Peters and Lauren Shearing, go and see them right now
  • C3467X - Hosts regular jams, organises workshops, performs longform.  Amazingly committed and organised.
  • Duck Duck Goose - a jam night hosted by Victoria Hogg and Waleed Akhtar
  • Friend of a Friend - Harold, all-women, "scenic group games", super-relaxed conversation with the audience as an opening
  • Impromptu Shakespeare - narrative
  • Improv Noir - narrative
  • The Inflatables - shortform
  • The Malakas - longform, strong groupmind
  • The Pioneers - Harold, stylised characters
  • The RH Experience - shortform, musical
  • The Scene - monoscenes, involved Paul Foxcroft
  • The Science of Living Things - montage, fast scenes and crisp French edits
  • Showstoppers - saw a scratch featuring Susan Harrison (2014), plus a full show in the Gilded Balloon some years back (2008)
  • We Want Information - longform, very aware of connection, bold choices totally accepted by troupe.
  • Wolfpack - my project with Jed Rose
  • Fat Kitten - shortform, led by James Ross, defunct.

Rest of UK

  • The Cambridge Impronauts - my first troupe ♥.  Mixture of shortform and narrative.
  • The Improverts - Edinburgh, shortform
  • The Maydays - Brighton, musical - saw them do a 'confessional' type show

Europe

  • The Barcelona Improv Group - speed Harolds, performed at CPH 2014, organise the Barcelona Improv Festival
  • Improkraatia - Tallinn, Estonia, performed their show Starve Trek at CPH 2014
  • La Théâtre de l'Oignon - Strasbourg, performed their show Random at Copenhagen Improv Festival (CPH) 2014.  Truly impressive command of atmosphere.

US

  • iO teachers - not a troupe as such, but Tara diFrancisco, Jet Eveleth and Lyndsay Hailey of iO performed a free-form longform at CPH 2014.
