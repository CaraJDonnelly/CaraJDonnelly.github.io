Gus, bored on the train to work, turned to the guy next to him and spoke.

“I'm Gus. Mind if I ask your name?”

The stranger looked confused. Gus persisted.

“Your name?”

“I'm, er, Death.” Death admitted. The Universe flickered; the carriage chilled significantly.

“You're just a man on a train,” said Gus, “Why would Death appear as just a man on a train?” He frowned. “Am I about to die?”

“No, no,” said Death, “I'm here because being 'Just a man on a train' is a Death of sorts.”
