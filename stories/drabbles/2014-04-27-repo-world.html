Angels exist outside of Time, the great branching artwork that splits and splits again. There is a branch where you chose differently, and one where the Axis powers won. It is a garden of forking paths, and the angels sculpt Time's bleeding edge. When each day is beautiful they add it to the branch.

Today was made in several parts, and Raphael is healing the conflicts. Uriel flutters his strawberry-blond wings and speaks.

"Samyaza is lacking. He creates, but never adds his days to the branch. He dances through the past, neglecting the future."

Raphael nods.

"Commitment issues."
